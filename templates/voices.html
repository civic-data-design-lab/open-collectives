We are sharing more than ever before, and collective living has been a common solution to our needs as a community. Collective sharing can help address many of our contemporary issues—housing affordability, economic crisis, the elder boom, reputational economy, and the future of work.

The Collective Voices platform seeks your input in envisioning answers to the question—how will we live together? Add your voice and explore what others think about the future of care, labor, co-living, and the market. We welcome your honesty and thoughtful consideration and encourage you to explore each theme further.
