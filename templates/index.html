Open Collectives

Open Collectives is an immersive installation featuring digital platforms and architectural projects that seek to leverage the power of solidarity in order to strengthen economic sovereignty, housing affordability, communal self-determination, and mutual aid.

Open Collectives offers new perspectives on pressing issues such as the future of work, the gig economy, elder boom, affordable housing, and widening inequality. Featured projects engage with the following themes:

Labor

Working with Mosaic.us—a full-stack construction company developing technologies to make home-building more efficient, inclusive, sustainable, and fulfilling.

Market

Assisting Quipu—a micro-currency digital platform—to establish a physical marketplace empowering members of a low-income community in Colombia.

Care

Supporting Carehaus in developing intergenerational co-housing where caregivers live alongside and care for older adults and disabled people.

Living

Developing new collective living models with Communit in retrofitted buildings and neighborhood-scale co-housing.
